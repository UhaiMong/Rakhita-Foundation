Who We Are

About Us

Rakhita Foundation is a humanitarian, non-profit organization committed to protecting and uplifting the suffering Arakan people affected by Myanmar junta brutality. Our work focuses on emergency response, medical aid, disaster relief, child support, displacement assistance, and long-term community development.

We aim to:

  • Deliver life-saving food, medicine, and emergency supplies
  • Support displaced families with shelter and safety
  • Provide education assistance for affected children
  • Promote awareness and advocacy for human rights
  • Strengthen communities through sustainable development programs

Rakhita Foundation stands as a beacon of hope, working tirelessly to rebuild lives and empower the Arakan community with resilience, security, and dignity.
